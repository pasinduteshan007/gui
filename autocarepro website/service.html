AutoCare Pro AUTO PARTS MARKET PLACE

Find spare parts for your car

AutoCare Pro has launched a specialized online marketplace for spare parts dealers that offers car owners a convenient way to find authentic OEM or OES spare parts. Previously, car owners would spend a significant amount of time searching for spare parts by walking around the streets of Panchikawatta and Delkanda.

With this e-commerce platform, car owners can access a wide range of top-quality spare parts and make purchases online, with delivery included.

The primary objective of this marketplace is to protect car owners from purchasing fake spare parts at inflated prices and to promote the sale of genuine spare parts at a reasonable cost.
